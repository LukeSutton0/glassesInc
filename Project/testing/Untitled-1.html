'''Combat Rift''' Combat Rift is a RPG simulator created and published by Crafty Savage! on the platform Roblox. Released on 7th of April 2021. '''Gameplay''' You begin your adventure with a wooden sword, you can start combat right away and can begin the grind! Throughout your adventure you'll encounter different kinds of mobs stretched across the different zones and even bosses to rile up the challenge. Become the very best by upgrading your backpack in the shops, grind for weapons dropped by the very mobs that you kill, and even rank up to show off your dedication and hard work! And for those adventures who hate to travel alone, there are pets in every stage for you to hatch and grow even more powerful.
